Welcome to Your Fujitsu Apprentice Portal

Helpful information to support you throughout your apprenticeship journey

📝 Your Checklist

This is where your onboarding begins. Here's what you should aim to complete early on:

  • ✅ Submit your ID and eligibility documents
  • ✅ Complete your Right to Work check
  • ✅ Schedule your onboarding call
  • ✅ Read the Welcome Pack and HR handbook
  • ✅ Accept your offer and contract

🤝 Meet Your Mentor

Each apprentice is assigned a Buddy and an HR Reviewer:

  • 👤 Your Buddy is another apprentice or colleague who can answer questions and share tips
  • 🧑‍💼 Your HR Reviewer supports you with goals, reviews, and wellbeing during your apprenticeship

Expect contact via email or Teams within your first few days!

📅 Important Dates

Keep track of the following milestones (dates vary by intake):

  • 📌 Online induction (Week 1)
  • 📌 Security briefing and mandatory training (Week 1–2)
  • 📌 Welcome webinar and team intro (Week 2)
  • 📌 First review with HR (Week 6)
